Welcome to the world's first Avatar extraction into the real world using spatial computing on the Magic Leap. With the Magic Leap you can experience 3D scanned avatars created by Bluevishunu right from your browser.

About the Technology

With the Helio Browser on the Magic Leap it provides the user the ability to extract 3D content into the world of spatial computing. The Avatar content was created and scanned by BlueVishnu 3D scanning technology. Engage VR partnered with BlueVishnu to create a Magic Leap Browser experience that allows users to view and interact with 3D Scanned High Resolution Avatars in a special way along with the future in mind to capture your personal 3D Avatar in Real Time.
